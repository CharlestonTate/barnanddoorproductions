Meet the Founder!

Jak Kristowski was born in Tampa Florida where he started making films and posting them on YouTube. He moved to South Carolina when he was 13, he started getting involved more with production companies and productions. He started a Film Club where he directed a film Called Julia Legare.

At the age 18, he decided to start his own production company called Barn Door Productions. His company has produced, directed, and casted multiple different productions.


What is Barn Door Productions?

Barn Door Productions is a production company based in Charleston South Carolina, the company was founded in 2022 by Jak Kristowski. They have worked in multiple genres, such as TV, film, music, videos, online multimedia. some of our credits are working with the kidfacechance, and working on Spider-Man: the dark age.
